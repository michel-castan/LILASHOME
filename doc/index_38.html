BoutonsLoadSaveMemory

The item "BoutonsSaveMemory" is a panel with two buttons, load and save, allowing initialization of a memory from the contents of a text file. The attributes of this element are:

  • memory : Indicates the instance MemoryDisplayable
  • memInit : indicates the resource (extension .mem) containing the image memory.

Example :

<awt layout="border" name="pan"> <page_start layout="border"> <center layout="scroll" width="300" height="300"> <MemoryDisplay memory="MemoireRAM.mem" model="ModeleMemoireAdrOctet" sizeCol0="20" sizeCol1="50"/> </center> <page_end> <BoutonsLoadSaveMem memory="MemoireRAM.mem" memInit="lilas.bib.logic.memoire.memoireInit"/> </page_end> </page_start> </awt>

Memory Image file format:

<MemoryImage> <Word adr="5" val="content of the word 5"/> <Word val="content of the word 6"/> <Word val="content of the word 7"/> <Word adr="15" val="content of the word 15"/> <Word val="content of the word 16"/> <Word val="content of the word 17"/> </MemoryImage>
corresponds to the memory initialization with the following content:
adr 5 : content of the word 5 adr 6 : content of the word 6 adr 7 : content of the word 7 adr 8 : content of the word 7 adr 9 : content of the word 7 ... adr 13 : content of the word 7 adr 14 : content of the word 7 adr 15 : content of the word 15 adr 16 : content of the word 16 adr 17 : content of the word 17 adr 17 : content of the word 17 adr 17 : content of the word 17 ...
